Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse

"Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse" is an upcoming animated superhero film set to be released in 2023.

The movie is a sequel to the critically acclaimed and Oscar-winning film "Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse" released in 2018, Directed by Joaquim Dos Santos.
the sequel is highly anticipated by fans and promises to continue the groundbreaking and visually stunning storytelling of its predecessor.
"Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse" is expected to explore new dimensions of the Spider-Verse and introduce audiences to different versions of Spider-Man from alternate universes.
The film will likely follow Miles Morales, the Afro-Latino Spider-Man, as he embarks on another epic adventure through the multiverse, encountering diverse versions of the beloved hero.
